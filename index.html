TP questions§ tap to expand

Questions.

Do I need BPO experience?add

No. We value language skills and communication. Full paid training — 2 to 6 weeks — before you handle any customer interactions.

Do you support visa and work permit?add

Yes — for candidates relocating to Thailand, we support the Non-B visa and work permit, processed at company cost.

What languages are you hiring for?add

Mainly Thai and English, with multilingual lines opening regularly.

What's the salary range?add

Competitive with the Thai market, plus allowances and bonuses. Your recruiter shares the range early. Final terms are confirmed in your offer letter.

What does a typical workday look like?add

8-hour shifts with rest days each week. Pantries, prayer/quiet rooms and break areas on-site. BTS-connected for an easy commute.

Is Bangkok affordable?add

Yes — one of Asia's most liveable cities. Great street food, efficient transit, and a strong international community.

Can I grow into leadership?add

Many team leads started frontline. Structured promotions, mentorship, leadership academies and GoFluent training.
